This is information graph and have an interactive function.
this is programmed in javascript, using d3 library and Jquery.
Let me teach you how to use our graph. It is simple.

Select group-tags located in the left side.
then several behavior patterns are drawn according to tags.

X-axis show time and Y-axis show percentage of each behavior. fundamentally, graph is based on 'Time Use Survey' conducted by Korean Statistical Office in 2009.

The Bar graph on the right side shows you the number of people that you select. If there are many sample, bar graph reach high position and has blue hue. on the other hand, the less people you selected, the bar reach low position, and has red hue. (warning message that this result isn't reliable)

Through this interactive graph, you can easily see the life style of each group.
